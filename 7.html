Project Details

Gensuite is a cloud based Environment, Health and Safety (EHS) software platform originally built for GE Power Systems. With a broad array of products & services, they currently support over 1 million users worldwide.

During my time at Gensuite I worked on a variety of projects from UI design and prototypes to print design. I also worked with many different teams at once, balancing different project requirements and deadlines.
