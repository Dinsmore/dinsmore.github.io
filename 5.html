Project Details

Founded in the late 1800's Jacob Masonry is a multi-generational commercial masonry company serving the Greater Cincinnati Tri-State Area.

Taking inspiration from stonework, this single page site uses project images as blocks, creating layers between the text and contact form. An integrated employment application reduces the need for potential job seekers to download paper forms.
