Project Details

Full Court Challenge started out as a side project with Tyler Gross to see if we could build a better March Madness bracket. Back in 2011 most brackets were made in Flash or only showed each region. We wanted to see if we could build the full bracket completely out of HTML, CSS, and jQuery.
